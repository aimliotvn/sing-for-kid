The Karaoke program for family and kids on YouTube karaoke videos

What is SafeFam?
SafeFam is a Karaoke program allowing family and kids sing Karaoke on YouTube. It has been designed to empower them to better learn Vietnamese words through singing.

Why did we create SafeFam?
Because YouTube kaoraoke videos are made for adults who know how to communicate verbally, an under-10 audience cannot read neither speak many words. We decided to step in and create an innovative karaoke platform dedicated to kids to help them foster the learning effectively.
